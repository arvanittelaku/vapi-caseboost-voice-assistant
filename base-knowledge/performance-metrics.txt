## VAPI Success Metrics

### Call Performance Metrics
- **Call Volume**: Number of calls received per day/week/month
- **Call Completion Rate**: Percentage of calls completed vs. dropped
- **Average Call Duration**: Time spent on each call
- **Call Quality Score**: Overall call quality rating

### Lead Qualification Metrics
- **Qualification Rate**: Percentage of calls resulting in qualified leads
- **Practice Area Accuracy**: Correct identification of legal specialty
- **Budget Qualification**: Successful budget range identification
- **Urgency Assessment**: Accurate urgency level detection

### Conversion Metrics
- **Call to Appointment Rate**: Percentage of calls converting to scheduled appointments
- **Appointment to Lead Rate**: Percentage of appointments converting to qualified leads
- **Lead to Client Rate**: Percentage of leads converting to paying clients
- **Overall Conversion Rate**: End-to-end conversion from call to client

### Customer Experience Metrics
- **Customer Satisfaction Score**: Post-call feedback ratings
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Likelihood to recommend CaseBoost
- **First Call Resolution**: Issues resolved in single call
- **Response Time**: Time to respond to customer inquiries
